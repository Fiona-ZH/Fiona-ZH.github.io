ABOUT ME
I’m a Product Designer with a background in engineering and a deep commitment to human-centered design. My unconventional path—from scientific research to product design—taught me to approach challenges with analytical rigor while staying adaptable, a mindset that fuels continuous learning and growth.
Crafting impactful experiences
Over the past seven years, I’ve designed and launched intuitive digital products across diverse industries, bringing creativity, technology, and business needs into alignment. I see design not as a static discipline, but as a process of constant discovery.
My design philosophy
Leveraging AI as a mindset has become central to my practice—it extends creativity, accelerates exploration, and helps uncover new possibilities. For me, great design is about more than delivering seamless experiences; it’s about empowering teams to think differently and creating solutions that genuinely improve people’s lives.
